LHDC T&C

Our Terms & Conditions

1. All communication, written and oral, shall be English.
‍
2. The contracting authority/sponsor shall accept all entries submitted, address all cases of unfair advantage that may emerge and apply sanctions for established cases of fraud in accordance with provisions of these guidelines.
‍
3. Only one submission may be entered per participant except stated otherwise in the specific competition programme.
‍
4. It is the competitor’s obligation/responsibility to ensure his/her entry is received intact at the submission venue. The sponsor bears no responsibility for defects arising in transit.
‍
5. By making a submission, a competitor commits to adhering to the guidelines, submission requirements and to sharing the copyright of its design with the sponsor that may photograph, print or publish for archival, educational or publicity purposes.
‍
6. It is the competitor’s obligation to arrange for the retrieval of non-winning entries where applicable.
‍
7. There shall be no communication with the Jury during deliberations except authorized persons.  Once the competition programme /booklet is issued, contact with the organizers will be permitted only through the channels provided by the guidelines.
‍
8. All finalists must be up to date financial members of NIA Lagos Chapter.
‍
9. Applicants must not be above 35 years of age.
‍
10. Groups can apply but as one entity and the cash prize will be given to the group, not individuals.
‍
11. All students must present a Letter of Reference or Attestation from their schools.
